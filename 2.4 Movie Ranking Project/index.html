Best Healer Blades in Xenoblade Chronicles 2

Graded in Terms of Availability and Usability

1. Boreas

Pretty standard wind type blade that you can get through the gacha mechanic of the game. Boreas has decent healing and is viable until endgame.

Not to mention boreas has the field skill Salvage Mastery which benefits Rex when diving for gear

2. Nim

Another gacha blade which has good healing properties. Nim heals the party through blade skills which will always activate her healing. Nim is a good option when you want to dish out damage while also healing your party

To add, Nim has the Earth Mastery property which is a good field skill to have in the Titan of Mor Ardain

3. Ursula

Ursula is a rare blade that can be obtained through the gacha system. She excels in healing and support, making her a valuable asset to any team. Ursula's healing abilities are top-notch, and she can also boost the party's stats with her skills.

Moreover, Ursula has the field skill Harmony, which is useful for various quests and challenges throughout the game.
